About Us

Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador program of Sri Ramakrishna Engineering College is a community started by the students, for the students and to the students of SREC. The vision and mission of MLSA SREC is to pave a way for the innovation of the students and to provide an environment to improvise their technical knowledge by practically working on it. We have two separate departments where one concentrates on conducting regular events like hackathons , ideation contests and other non technical events . The other is the Core team which deals with handling projects and to teach students by conducting workshops and seminars on their respective donations.
